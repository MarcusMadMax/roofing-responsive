Residential Roof Installation

New roofs for new builds, extensions and renovations

Protect your new home or extension with a quality long run roof, professionally installed by the experienced team of Auckland roofers from Roof Me. We provide new roof installation for new homes, extensions and garages. We understand that each home, location and environment is unique, so our customised roofing solutions are designed to complement your building and your budget, while taking these important factors into consideration. Roofing & Cladding Profiles

At Roof Me we only use high quality Roofing products from the COLORSTEEL® and COLORCOTE® Range. Roofing products may look the same in appearance but they may not perform the same due to different grades (thickness), coatings (paint), profile and even what type of metallic make up they are. All our materials are backed by our Suppliers and also carry their own manufacture warranties. When choosing a Roofing Product there are many factors that you need to consider the products effectiveness, durability, safety, life expectancy of the Roof – we will make sure we quote the right materials are being used to complete the job to the highest possible standards. We have options available for every budget as affordability is also a main factor. Let us help you find the perfect Roofing Solution for your Home.

CORRUGATE

An ageless Icon in the roofing industry with the profile offering flexibility in the designing of modern buildings, and is supplied as both a standard cover and extra wide cover.

MULTIDEK®

A strong, bold European style roofing and cladding which is attached to roof supports using a concealed clip fastening system. Multidek’s wider coverage eliminates laps and saves on installation costs.

MULTIRIB®

A bold and aesthetically pleasing profile designed primarily for the commercial/industrial roofing and cladding market. The profile exhibits exceptional strength and water carrying capacity.

RIBLINE® 800

An aesthetically pleasing profile designed for both residential and commercial roofing and cladding. A wide cover ensures economy, fewer laps and ease of installation.

RT7®

A medium height trapezoidal profile which has an exceptional strength to weight ratio. Impressive spanning capability, water carrying capacity and at a relatively low roof pitch. RT7® is equally impressive in a commercial or industrial roofing and cladding application.

TRIMRIB®

TRIMRIB® is manufactured and supplied as a series of aesthetically pleasing residential and commercial roofing and cladding profiles supplied with three design options.
